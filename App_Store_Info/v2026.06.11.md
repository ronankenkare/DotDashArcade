### Description
Dot Dash Arcade is a simple arcade game that tests your timing and precision.
Tap the screen when the moving dot aligns with the highlighted dash to score points — but miss, and it's game over.

Each tap brings a new challenge as the speed increases, demanding sharper focus and perfect rhythm.
Play in Classic Mode for a steady challenge, or step up to Advanced Mode, where the target shrinks as the pace quickens.

Designed with a clean, minimal interface, Dot Dash Arcade is an easy-to-learn, hard-to-master game perfect for quick sessions or endless high-score chases.

**Features:**
- Simple one-tap gameplay
- Two dynamic modes: Classic & Advanced
- Sleek, minimal design
- Local best score tracking

How far can your reflexes take you?
